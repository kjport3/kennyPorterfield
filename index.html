About me

Hi there! My name is Kenny Porterfield and I am a web developer living in Atlanta, Georgia. When I'm not busy coding, I like to spend my time running, reading, and learning. Fun facts: I have run an average of over 2,000 miles a year for the last 5 years, and before that in 2014 I thru-hiked the Appalachian Trail. I have read 61 books in the last year. And I am currently a Senior Email Developer at Digital Additive and am a Salesforce Marketing Cloud certified Email Specialist.

I think one of the best ways to learn is to teach. Not that I think I'm one of the best developers around and have so much to teach everybody, but because it forces me to spend the time to try to understand how and why things work better than at just a surface level. You have to if you're going to write something about it! So that's what I try to do with my blog (as well as share hopefully interesting life stuff). When I'm working my way through a problem or learning a new thing, I try to write a blog post about it.
